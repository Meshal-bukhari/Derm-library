KFSD: The Scarring Trio Board Review

Master This Rare Cicatricial Alopecia in 5 Minutes!
🧠 MEMORY BLAST: "K-FS-D = Kids + Follicular Scarring → Decalvans (hair loss)"
🧬

Genetics & Inheritance

  • 🔹 X-linked dominant (most common)
  • 🔹 Autosomal dominant
  • 🔹 Sporadic cases
💡 BOARD PEARL: Girls in X-linked cases have milder presentation!
👶

Age & Demographics

  • 🔹 Starts: Early childhood
  • 🔹 Progression: Face → Trunk → Extremities
  • 🔹 Pustular variant: Around puberty
🎯 HIGH-YIELD TRIAD: "S-E-C"
Scarring alopecia + Eyelash/eyebrow loss + Corneal dystrophy (boys)

Disease Progression Timeline

Stage 1: Face

Begins with keratosis pilaris on face

Fine follicular papules

Stage 2: Spread

Progresses to trunk & extremities

Follicular plugging increases

Stage 3: Hair Loss

Cicatricial alopecia develops

Scalp, eyebrows, eyelashes affected

Stage 4: Complications

Corneal dystrophy (boys)

Photophobia develops

High-Yield Differentials

Keratosis pilaris atrophicans faciei
• Also called ulerythema ophryogenes
• Similar but limited to face
• No widespread scarring alopecia
Lichen planopilaris
• Adult onset
• Violaceous papules
• Perifollicular scale
Graham-Little syndrome
• Triad: Scalp alopecia + follicular KP + non-scarring axillary/pubic alopecia
• Adult onset
KID syndrome
• Keratitis + Ichthyosis + Deafness
• Palmoplantar keratoderma
• Hearing loss is key differentiator
💊

Treatment Strategy

🎯 REMEMBER: "EVADE" - NO CURE!
Emollients + Vitamin A + Antibiotics + Dapsone + Early intervention
  • 🔹 Topical/systemic retinoids
  • 🔹 Keratolytics for hyperkeratosis
  • 🔹 Antibiotics for pustular eruptions
  • 🔹 Topical steroids for inflammation
  • 🔹 Consider laser hair removal

Board-Style Quick Quiz

Q: A 7-year-old boy presents with progressive hair loss, photophobia, and follicular papules on his face. What's the most likely diagnosis?

A: KFSD - The triad of scarring alopecia + corneal involvement + childhood onset = KFSD!

🏆 FINAL BOARD TIP: When you see scarring alopecia + childhood onset + eye involvement = Think KFSD!
