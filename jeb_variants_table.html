🩺 JEB Variants - Quick Review

Junctional Epidermolysis Bullosa for Dermatology MCQ Exam

🦷 UNIVERSAL FINDING: Dental Enamel Hypoplasia occurs EXCLUSIVELY in JEB - pitting of teeth surfaces → severe caries & premature tooth loss
Variant Name Defective Protein(s) Key Clinical Presentation & Systemic Memory Hooks & Figures
JEB, Severe
(JEB-Herlitz, EB Letalis) 		Laminin 332
(truncating mutations) 		Severe, generalized blistering from birth. Very poor prognosis, often fatal in infancy.

PATHOGNOMONIC: Excessive/Exuberant Granulation Tissue
Located symmetrically in periorificial areas, skin folds (axillae, groin), nape of neck, periungual sites.
Systemic: Severe malnutrition, anemia, tracheolaryngeal stenosis (fatal airway obstruction)
"Herlitz Hurts" 💀 Most severe form. The red, raw, "proud flesh" of exuberant granulation tissue is the key visual.
(Fig 32.6, 32.9)
JEB, Intermediate
(JEB-non-Herlitz, Generalized Atrophic Benign EB) 		Laminin 332 (milder mutations)
or Type XVII Collagen 		Generalized blistering, less severe than Herlitz. Patients survive into adulthood. Blistering improves but leaves atrophic scarring.

Does NOT feature exuberant granulation tissue.
Systemic: Milder complications; corneal scarring can occur
"Intermediate is In-Between" ⚖️ Not as severe as Herlitz, not as mild as localized. Think "atrophic" scarring as long-term clue.
JEB with Pyloric Atresia
(JEB-PA) 		α6β4 Integrin The Classic Dyad: Generalized blistering at birth + congenital pyloric atresia (non-bilious vomiting, feeding intolerance).
Systemic: JEB-PA is "much more common than EBS with pyloric atresia." Also: hydroureter and hydronephrosis
"Pyloric Atresia = α6β4" 🚨 Medical emergency combining dermatology and GI.
JEB-Laryngo-onycho-cutaneous
(LOC Syndrome) 		Laminin α3 chain
(α isoform) 		The Triad in the Name:
• Hoarse cry from laryngeal involvement (exuberant granulation tissue in larynx)
• Severe onychodystrophy
• Cutaneous erosions 		LOC = Larynx, Onycho, Cutaneous 🗣️ The hoarse cry in a blistering neonate is a major red flag.
JEB with Interstitial Lung Disease & Nephrotic Syndrome
 α3 Integrin subunit Severe, systemic variant defined by combination of blistering with progressive lung and kidney failure. The name describes the devastating systemic involvement 💔
JEB, Inversa
 Laminin 332 Disease activity primarily localized to intertriginous areas (axillae, groin). "Inversa = In the Folds" 🔄
JEB, Localized
 Laminin 332, Collagen XVII, α6β4 integrin, α3 integrin Mildest form, with blistering confined mainly to acral sites (hands, feet, knees, elbows). Similar distribution to EBS-localized but different cleavage level 🤲
