EBS, Localized (Weber-Cockayne) Acral blisters on hands/feet due to friction. Worse in warm weather. Most common form. "Weber-Walk-ayne": Walking → foot friction.
(Fig. 32.2)
EBS, Intermediate Generalized blistering on trunk, limbs, buttocks. Less severe than severe form. May have focal PPK. "More than walking" causes blisters.
(Fig. 32.3)
EBS, Severe (Dowling-Meara) Severe, generalized blisters at birth. Grouped/herpetiform clusters. Progressive PPK. Clumped keratin filaments ultrastructurally. "Dowling-Meara is Mean": Severe, early, grouped blisters.
(Figs. 32.7, 32.8)
