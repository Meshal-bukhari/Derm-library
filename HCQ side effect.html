Key Clinical Points:

• GI effects are the most common reason for early discontinuation • Retinopathy risk negligible for first 5 years at proper dosing (≤5.0 mg/kg) • HCQ generally better tolerated than CQ • Regular monitoring essential for long-term therapy

Dosing Reference: Recommended dose ≤5.0 mg/kg real body weight to minimize retinal toxicity
